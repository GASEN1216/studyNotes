# 1.knife4j接口文档只需要引入依赖，配置写在yml文件里即可
访问Knife4j的文档地址：`http://ip:port/doc.html`即可查看文档
# springdoc-openapi项目配置  
springdoc:  
  swagger-ui:  
    path: /swagger-ui.html  
    tags-sorter: alpha  
    operations-sorter: alpha  
  api-docs:  
    path: /v3/api-docs  
  group-configs:  
    - group: 'default'  
      paths-to-match: '/**'  
      packages-to-scan: com.gasen.findmeetbackend.controller
